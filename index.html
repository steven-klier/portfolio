Bio

Steven Klier is a guitarist, pianist, drummer, singer, producer, songwriter, composer, and lover of music. Guitar has been his main instrument for 12 years which has led to gaining experience playing and writing anything from acoustic finger-style to shred metal. Steven has been a part of jazz big bands, funk ensembles, death metal bands, cover bands, rock bands and has written music in most of them. Steven currently holds a Bachelors of Science in Audio Production and Technology with a minor in Music Composition from Michigan Technological University.
